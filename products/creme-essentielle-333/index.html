N° 333 — FAQ

What does it actually feel like?+

Silky and cushioned going on, gone in under a minute. The finish is soft-matte — conditioned skin, not coated skin. It was formulated to survive the real test: typing, phone screens, and handshakes right after applying.

Is it really fragrance-free — or just “unscented”?+

Truly fragrance-free. No perfume, and no masking fragrance hiding under “unscented.” What you may notice is the faint, natural scent of the plant oils themselves — which fades on skin.

Can I use it on my face?+

It’s formulated and tested as a hand & body cream — richer than a face formula needs to be. It’s gentle, but faces deserve their own chemistry: that’s exactly what La Crème Éclat and La Crème Calme are for. Join the list to hear when they arrive.

Is it suitable during pregnancy?+

The formula is fragrance-free and fully disclosed — every ingredient is listed above so you and your provider can review the complete list together. As with any skincare during pregnancy, we recommend consulting your provider; we’ll never make that call for you.

Is it halal?+

We formulate halal-conscious by design: vegetable-only glycerin, fermentation-derived hyaluronate, no animal-derived ingredients, no alcohol in the intoxicant sense (cetyl alcohol is a plant wax, chemically unrelated). We are not yet third-party certified — and we won’t use the word “certified” until we are.

How long does a jar last?+

With daily hand use plus a few body applications a week, most people finish 120 ml in roughly two to three months. Once opened, use within six months for peak freshness.
