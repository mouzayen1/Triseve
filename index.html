The method behind every TRISÈVE formula

Formulated in the open

Every brand lists its ingredients. We explain ours.

The ingredient list is required by law — that part isn’t transparency, it’s compliance. What we add is the part no one requires: what each ingredient does, where it comes from, and why it earned its place. And because “fragrance” is the one legal word allowed to conceal dozens of undisclosed chemicals, we simply never use it.

Read our standards

Synthetic fragrance

The legal loophole. One word can hide an entire undisclosed formula. We name everything instead.

Silicones

Borrowed slip. Ours comes from coco-caprylate — a coconut ester that does the job honestly.

Parabens & phenoxyethanol

We preserve with a radish-root ferment instead — effective, and easier to stand behind.

PEGs

Our emulsifier is an olive-derived pair, not an ethoxylated shortcut.

Mineral oil & petrolatum

Plant oils and squalane do the sealing — with an elegance petroleum never had.

Animal-derived ingredients

Vegetable glycerin, fermented hyaluronate. Cruelty-free and halal-conscious by design.
