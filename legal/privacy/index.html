Privacy policy

EFFECTIVE JULY 2026 · OMNIA WORKS LLC

The same rule as our labels: we tell you exactly what we collect and why. This policy explains our practices in plain English.

What we collect

When you place an order: your name, email, shipping address, and order details. Payment is processed entirely by our payment provider (Stripe) — we never see or store your card number. When you join our list: your email address. Like most sites, we also receive standard technical data (browser type, pages visited) via analytics.

How we use it

To fulfill and ship your orders, respond to your messages, send the emails you signed up for, and understand how the site is used so we can improve it. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it except with the service providers needed to run the store (payment processing, shipping, email delivery, analytics).

Email & unsubscribing

Every marketing email includes a one-click unsubscribe. Transactional emails (order and shipping confirmations) are sent as part of fulfilling your order.

Cookies & analytics

We use minimal cookies and analytics to keep the site working and understand traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your California rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents may request to know, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about them, and to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell or share it as defined by the CPRA). To exercise any right, email us — we will verify and respond within the statutory window. We never discriminate for exercising your rights.

Data retention & security

We keep order records as required for tax and legal purposes, and list data until you unsubscribe. Data is stored with reputable providers using industry-standard safeguards.

Children

Our products and site are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Contact

Questions or requests: email . OMNIA WORKS LLC, Newport Beach, California.
