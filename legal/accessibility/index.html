Accessibility

EFFECTIVE JULY 2026 · OMNIA WORKS LLC

Transparency includes access. This site is built toward WCAG 2.1 Level AA, and we treat accessibility as a standing commitment rather than a checkbox.

What we’ve built in

Semantic HTML and landmarks; full keyboard navigability with visible focus states; alt text on meaningful imagery; color contrast checked against our palette; support for reduced-motion preferences; forms with programmatic labels and live status messages.

Ongoing work

We audit new pages as they ship and re-test after significant changes. Accessibility is never “done,” and we expect to keep improving.

Found a barrier?

If any part of this site is difficult to use with your assistive technology, email and we will address it promptly — and gladly take your order or answer your question by email in the meantime.
